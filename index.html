About Us

Established in Tarawa, Kiribati, Island Stationery has grown into a multi-service business trusted by communities across the islands. Our mission is to provide quality stationery, efficient cargo services, and reliable inter-island logistics that support the daily needs and ambitions of individuals and businesses in Kiribati.

We are proud to be more than just a stationery shop. With our own shipping fleet and overseas cargo arrangements, we connect islands and help local families and businesses move their goods with ease and trust. Our resort in Abaiang also provides a peaceful escape for those seeking relaxation amidst the beauty of the Pacific.
